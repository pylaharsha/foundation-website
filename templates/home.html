Our Mission

We empower communities through compassionate service, cultural enrichment and educational support. From promoting arts and cultural exchange to nurturing ethical values and spiritual growth, our mission is to uplift lives and expand our impact across the Americas.

Our Four Pillars of Service

Rooted in compassion, guided by wisdom — building stronger communities across the Americas.

🤝

Charitable & Humanitarian

Providing food, clothing, shelter and medical assistance to those in need, at shelters and underserved communities.

🎨

Culture & Literature

Promoting music, art, dance, and literary awareness through exchange programs and community workshops.

📖

Philosophical & Ethical

Establishing coaching centers, providing scholarships and facilitating training programs for youth and children.

🌍

Community & Outreach

Establishing inclusive community spaces and outreach initiatives that foster harmony, tolerance, pluralism, and service.

Our Guiding Light

Dr. CBSatpathy — humanitarian, scholar, and inspirational leader touching millions across the globe.

Our Founder & Guide

Dr. Chandra Bhanu Satpathy

A noted scholar, author, and humanitarian — Dr. CBSatpathy embodies the noble principles of love, empathy, compassion, and devotion. Under his inspiration, more than two hundred centers devoted to philanthropy, community development, and human welfare are active across India, USA, Canada, Australia, South Africa, UK, and Europe.

He was invited by the US House of Representatives to open its session as a Guest Chaplain on June 24, 2015 — the first Indian citizen ever to receive such an honor.
