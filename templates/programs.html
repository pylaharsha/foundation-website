Our Programs

Serving communities with compassion, culture, and purpose

01

Charitable & Humanitarian Initiatives

  • Providing food, clothing, shelter & medical assistance to those in need and at homeless shelters
  • Book Drive by Kids — Collection and distribution of books through school entities & directly to needy learners
  • Food Drive by Youths — Collection from restaurants, food preparation, distribution at shelter homes, food banks & needy
  • Sports for Underserved Youths — Collection of sports accessories, distribution, coaching & mentorship
02

Promotion of Culture & Literature

  • Programs for cultural exchange and spreading literary awareness
  • Vocal & Instrumental Music — Platform for kids & others to learn
  • Art Workshops — Exploring diverse art forms & techniques
  • Literary Work — Creation of library & distribution of books
  • Dance Forms — Indian classical, western dance forms & folk dances
03

Philosophical & Ethical Literature

  • Establishment of coaching centers for kids and youth
  • Providing scholarships and facilitating training programs
  • Sai Divine Magazine — Spreading philosophical and spiritual teachings
  • Sai Ki Palki — Community celebration and spiritual gathering
  • Guru Bhagwat Parayan — Devotional readings and spiritual education
04

Advancement of Religion

  • Establishing centers for religious worship across the Americas
  • Providing religious instruction for families and youth
  • Missionary outreach and community engagement
  • Promoting interreligious dialogue and harmony
  • Expanding all programs to different parts of the Americas
